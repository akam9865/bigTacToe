these are the rules

  • Objective: get three large boxes in a row on the large board.
  • Score a large box by winning the small board within that large box.
  • X moves first, and can choose any small board to play his move, choosing any small box.
  • Subsequent moves must be played within the large box which corresponds to the previously played small box.
  • Example: X plays his first move within the center large box, in the top right small box. O's next move must be played in the top right large box.
  • If the corresponding large box is unplayable, the player may choose any large box to play.
